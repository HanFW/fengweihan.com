IBeeHub Portfolio Management Platform

Manage companies and talents portfolios for tech startups incubator

In summer 2017, I interned in a tech startup incubator - iBeeHub for a month, then attended a UX summer course at Peking University. As I happened to know that iBeeHub had a new project idea of creating a portfolio management platform, I volunteered to design this system for the company both as their project proposal and as my individual assignment for the summer course. I would like to thank the company for giving me such a great opportunity and thank all the amazing colleagues who helped a lot with the usability test of this project.

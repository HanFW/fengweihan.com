Global Smoking Trend

How has the global smoking trend changed over the years (1980-2012)?

The increasing concerns for coronavirus caused us to pay much more attention to health issues, especially pneumonia. Pneumonia is a disease caused by the coronavirus. When we were researching the main factors that cause pneumonia, we found smoking to be one of the largest factors. Therefore, we went on to search for datasets on smoking, and we found our datasets on IHME. We believe that this is an important topic to analyze because smoking has been negatively affecting so many people in the world. It does not only hurt the lungs, but also a lot of other organs.

Parisa Wang

Designed landing pages for collaboration handbag marketing campaign.

Parisa Wang partnered with CareerTu to design a collaboration handbag. This handbag was designed to “empower women in the workplace, unlock their potential, and celebrate their unique journey, in life & in love.” I designed the landing pages for the handbag release marketing campaign.

Background: Parisa Wang is a popular handbag brand in New York City. CareerTu is an online education platform that help young professionals in the tech field to unlock their potential.

Design Goals

Phase 1: design the “waitlist” landing page posted on CareerTu’s website, and have 150 waitlist subscribers by the launch day.

Phase 2: design the “pre-order” landing page posted on Parisa Wang’s website, and have 50 new bag in sales.

Market Research

User Research

By collaborating with the marketing team, we determined that the customers we would like to target are young female professionals who just entered the workplace or are early in their career.

Customer Interview

I conducted interviews with 5 individuals to understand people’s handbag shopping habits. Insights I got from the interviews:
* Customers are usually get attracted by the design of the bag first
* Price is an attractor
* Customers want to establish an overall understanding of the brand’s identity
* Customers want to know the size, materials and other details
* Customers like to see rich image content (different models wearing the bag, bags matching different outfits, how much the bag can pack)

Customer Journey Map

Customer Experience Flow

I collaborated with the marketing team to map out the end-to-end flow of customer experience throughout the marketing campaign process. That determines the customer’s experience before and after the landing pages.

Hi, this is Fengwei

I am a second-year Master's student studying User Experience and Information Architecture at University of Washington. Before this, I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Information Systems from National University of Singapore. While learning how to implement complicated systems, I found myself always wondering how my system actually helps people, and fascinated by creating effective solutions for users. That desire drove me closer to the roots of people's problems and needs, and led me into the field of UX. I want to make sure that things are created in a meaningful way, to solve the right problems and help those in need.
